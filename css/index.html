My thoughts on fashion

Fashion is something that has always been there and that will always be there. People are always going to want to dress well, and everyone needs clothes to dress up into. I think fashion has somewhat taken a full circle because the way people used to dress back in the 90s is the way we have started dressing now again with maybe a bit more of a modern style. Fashion always comes back!
